-Cross referenced calculated stats with in-game numbers to check accuracy; solo Laevatain (P0, Lv90)
 + SIG (No dupes, 7/7/4 Essence) with Tide Fall Light Armour, Hot Work Gauntlets, Hot Work Pyrometer x2
 (base, no artificing) against Rhodagn. Basic Attack 1 was used, with a hardcoded value due to not
 having Lv10 values at the time of testing, and the result was 960. The calculated value was rounded
 to the nearest integer instead of rounding down to accurately reflect the number in-game. Both the value
 in-game and the calculated value were 960.
-Yvonne tested (Lv90, 50% Trust, Mi Security 4/4 Max Artifice Stat 1 and 3, Wedge No Dupe, (7,6,3), Lv80).
Damage calculated was within 0.99% of what in-game display was (1179.3919199999998 vs 1180).
-Further Yvonne testing showed that the MI Security Stack system applies attack buff *AFTER*
attack crits. For example, if an attack crits, that attack does *NOT* benefit from the attack
buff stack but the next attacks do. (Basic Attack 1 will benefit from 2 stacks, but the crit
will proc a third stack, which the basic attack will not have benefitted from).
